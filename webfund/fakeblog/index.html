Mark Watney

Mark Watney has a degree in Botany which naturally made him a prime candidate for a mission to Mars. Little did he know that his Botany degree would play a key role in his survival after being left for dead on the 4th rock from the sun. A star he lovingly calls, Sol. Here is a very short summary of his time on Mars.

Date Range Sol Range Summary
11-07-2035 to 11-11-2035 1 to 5 Mission goes as planned
11-12-2035 to 12-05-2035 6 to 29 Sand storm causes all hell to break loose. Mark is left for dead and has to figure how he's going to survive.
12-06-2035 to 05-20-2036 30 to 191 Mark adjusts to surviving on Mars and manages to establish comms with Earth. They begin to formulate a plan to get Mark home.
05-21-2036 to 02-08-2037 192 to 448 Watney conducts preparations to travel to Schiaparelli Crater, where he can launch the Ares 4 MAV to rendezvous with Hermes.
02-09-2037 to 04-07-2037 449 to 505 Watney travels to Schiaparelli Crater, where he can launch the Ares 4 MAV to rendezvous with Hermes.
04-08-2037 to 05-22-2037 506 to 548 Watney conducts preparations to leave Mars aboard the Ares 4 MAV and rendezvous with Hermes.
05-23-2037 549 Watney launches in the Ares 4 MAV and is rescued by the Hermes crew.

  • 07-07-2035 - Ares 3 launches from Earth
  • 11-07-2035 - Ares 3 lands on Mars
  • 11-12-2035 - Ares 3 crew is forced to leave Mars without Mark Watney due to a severe dust storm.
  • 02-13-2036 - Watney reestablishes contact with Earth using the Pathfinder probe.
  • 02-09-2037 - Watney leaves Ares 3 site and travels to Ares 4 site
  • 04-07-2037 - Watney arrives at Ares 4 site
  • 05-23-2037 - Watney launches in the Ares 4 MAV and leaves Mars
